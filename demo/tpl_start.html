SPARQL Micro-Services Demo Page

This page demonstrates the use of SPARQL micro-services [1][2] to integrate, within a single SPARQL query, data from the TAXREF-LD Linked Data taxonomic register [3] with photos from Flickr, articles from the Biodiversity Heritage Library, traits from the Encyclopedia of Life trait bank, audio recordings from the Macaulay Library, and music tunes from MusicBrainz. The SPARQL results are transformed into HTML using the SPARQL Template Transformation Language [4].
